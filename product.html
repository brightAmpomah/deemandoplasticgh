Product Description

Deemado Company Limited offers a comprehensive range of high quality plastic and rubber products designed to meet the needs of retail, industrial, and commercial sectors. Its HM Roll provides high strength, tear resistant material for heavy duty packaging and transport, while LD Roll offers a lightweight, flexible, and stretchable solution for everyday packaging of medium weight goods.
Poly bags offer flexible, dust and moisture resistant packaging for small to medium items, while packing bags provide additional strength and flexibility for organised storage and secure transportation.
